Programs

Girl Hacks provides a channel of access through which participants can engage in a variety of personal and professional development education. They will be offered scholarships different workshops, trainings and certification course which include areas of focus such as: discovering and cultivating unique gifts and passions, setting career goals, digital marketing, coding, financial tools, health and wellness, sexual awareness, drug awareness, and navigating the transition into womanhood with grace.

Our work instills in girls a code of confidence that gives them the capacity to express agency in decision-making as they establish and act on goals for themselves. Through these trainings and workshops, Girl-hackers get to practice empowered decision-making, using their voice and discernment to influence key choices in their lives, being aware of, and able to act upon, a full array of possibilites.

Girl Hacks provides in-person and online pre and post integration support for the transformational trainings our participants are sponsored through.

Girl Hacks is a non-profit organization providing access to life-transforming programs, tools and education that inspire girls to be confident in making healthy choices for their lives in pursuit of their dreams.
