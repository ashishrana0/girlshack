What We Do

Our mission is to provide 17-22 year old girls access to community support, personal and professional resources they need to navigate their lives from a place of sovereignty, self-awareness and discernment. We have seen time and again that this has a positive ripple effect that benefits the collective, and every person whose life is touched by the transformation and empowerment that occurs on these girls paths. Over the past decade, many research studies such as this one have been conducted that demonstrate profound evidence that when girls are empowered to live to their full potential, everyone benefits exponentially.

These programs teach young women to use their own natural female strength to stand up for themselves as women, find their voice, and conquer fears.

Girl Hacks is a non-profit organization providing access to life-transforming programs, tools and education that inspire girls to be confident in making healthy choices for their lives in pursuit of their dreams.
